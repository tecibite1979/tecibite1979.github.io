Werewolf: The Apocalypse – Heart of the Forest is the first-ever video game based on the legendary tabletop RPG. You play as Maria, a young woman who has just arrived in Poland and is looking for her roots however as events unfold, she is drafted into an ancient war. The game is played as an aesthetic visual novel where Rage and Willpower are the major players. As you make different choices in the game, the story is formed around you.

The game’s primary resource is Rage and by controlling it, you can change Maria’s perspective and see aspects that are otherwise invisible if your Rage in not controlled well enough. The game is set in the Bialowieza Forest a.k.a. the Puszcza, the last primal wilderness in Europe. The game’s story is inspired by the 2016-2017 protests against commercial logging of the forest which resulted in a judiciary ban on deforestation.

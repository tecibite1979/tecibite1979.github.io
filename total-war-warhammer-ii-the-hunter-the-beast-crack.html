DLC TypeLord Pack
Release Date11 September, 2019

The Hunter and the Beast (stylised as The Hunter & The Beast) is a paid DLC Lord Pack for Total War: Warhammer II. It released on September 11th, 2019 alongside The Empire Undivided Update.

The pack adds Nakai the Wanderer leading the Spirit of the Jungle faction of Lizardmen, and Markus Wulfhart leading the Huntsmarshal's Expedition faction of The Empire.

This is the first paid DLC for TWW2 to feature content for races/factions introduced in TWW1.

Exercise Your Brain by Using It To Solve Problems

A body builder in gym tries to lift a heavy weight in his hands to make the muscle of his arm strong in order to enable him to lift heavier weights. The same is the case with brain power. Brain power can also enhanced by exercising it frequently.

Normally every activity of our life needs usage of our brain and serves as an exercise in itself for the brain. Still there are some activities which really engage our brain power– such activities may be routine activities but serves as better exercise for enhancing our brain power. Try to engage your brain power in such things more and more on regular basis. It really helps in sharpening your mind with the passage of time.
Some of such activities can be

  1. Learning a new thing, language or games really helps in awakening the sleeping faculties of your brain.
  2. Various games like puzzle games, chess, snooker and scrabble
  3. Solving analytical problems, mind provoking issues etc
  4. Making certain hand-made things, sceneries, painting etc.
  5. For students - Trying to learn difficult and more difficult subject, writing explanations and essays about various topics on regular basis.

Think Critically and Logically – Have A Scientific Thinking Style

The power of your brain depends on the way you use your brain to think, understand, interpret, analyze and observe things. Most of the people don’t have proper approach towards thinking approach. They may think about the apparent dimensions of things but ignore their unseen aspects. They may observe thing very externally. It is not a good habit. If you want to enhance your brain power and sharpen your mind, you should always think critically and logically about things. You should think rationally about things. It is called a scientific thinking style to deal with things. For instance, you need to solve an issue. Consider all of its dimensions, like the causes of problem, impacts of problem, and its various possible solutions for this problem. Never devise a solution to the problem without thinking rationally and logically about it, because if you do so your suggested solution may create more problems than the current one. So it is better to think from every angle considering all the facts about the problem and then select a solution that can solve the problem without creating any other problem. Apple juice, tomatoes, nuts and grain are also good for this purpose. Always have a good meal at the start of the day - the breakfast, It really helps a lot.

Daily Physical Exercise Is Essential For Brain Function – Daily Walk or Running

Physical exercise speeds up the circulation of blood to all parts of body including the brain. Oxygenated blood has a lot of nutrient to feed the brain. Therefore, physical exercise is very much essential for brain health. Getting physical exercise enhances the brain power by strengthening the brain cells and their connections in the brain.

Practicing Meditation helps in enhancing brain power

There are various ways and exercise of meditation which are quite helpful for enhancing your brain power. For example, the most commonly used way of meditation is setting at a calm place, relaxing your body, inhaling a deep breath into your lungs and exhaling your breathe slowly and getting focused on the flow of your breathe. It really helps you in sharpening your mind. Human brain, like other organs of body, needs proper rest after being used the whole day. Performing activities of whole day exhaust the brain and affect the function of brain.
